About Me

Rachel Wang

Hi! I'm Rachel Wang — a competitive water polo player who has been in the water as long as I can remember. I play for the Stanford Water Polo Club and am on the PAC Zone roster for the Olympic Development Program.

This summer, I want to give back by coaching girls ages 6–15 in swimming fundamentals and water polo — completely free of charge. Whether you've never been in a pool or you're already swimming, there's a place for you.

Sessions take place in South Bay, CA. You provide the pool — I bring the coaching, the drills, and the fun!
